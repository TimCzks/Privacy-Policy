# Privacy Policy for Aftermath Mobile

**Last updated:** 5th of January 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how **Aftermath Mobile** (“the App”) handles user information.  
The App is developed and provided by the developer (“we”, “us”, or “our”).

We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting it.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Data

**Aftermath Mobile** does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

### 2.2 Game Data (Local Storage Only)

The App stores game progress data **only on the user’s device**.  
This data is used exclusively to ensure proper gameplay functionality.

- No game data is stored on external servers  
- No game data is shared with the developer  
- No cloud storage is used  

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App displays **in-game advertisements** provided by third-party services.

These third-party ad providers may collect data independently in accordance with their own privacy policies.

### Third-party services used:

- **Unity Ads**  
  Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

- **ironSource (LevelPlay)**  
  Privacy Policy: https://developers.is.com/ironsource-mobile/air/ironsource-mobile-privacy-policy/

We do not control and are not responsible for how these third-party services collect or use data.

---

## 4. Advertising and Data Usage

The App itself does not provide personal data to advertisers.  
However, ad providers may automatically collect certain information, such as:

- Device information  
- Advertising identifiers  
- Approximate location (non-precise)  
- Ad interaction data  

This data collection is handled solely by the advertising SDKs and follows their respective privacy policies.

---

## 5. Children’s Privacy

**Aftermath Mobile** is not specifically designed for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.  
If you believe that a child has provided personal data through a third-party service used in this App, please contact the respective service provider directly.

---

## 6. Data Security

Because the App does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no external storage of user information that could be compromised by the developer.

Local game data remains entirely on the user’s device.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR or CCPA).  
Since the App does not collect personal data directly, requests regarding personal data should be directed to the relevant third-party service providers listed above.
